# Business model

Inspired by Wordle: a fun side project, one puzzle a day, no monetisation — that
went on to be acquired by a major media company.

## Monetisation approach

There is _no_ plan to monetise this app. The main purpose is to entertain me and
my friends, explore technologies and build a showcase for future employers. If
an external opportunity presents itself for monetisation, then maybe, but not a
goal per se.

## Constraints that affect product decisions

I cannot afford massive hosting fees, domains or advertising at this time.

## How business context shapes priorities

It could be fun to watch this grow, but not at the cost of the core experience.
Building a community and getting word of mouth is a priority.

Simplicity is key. Sign-in is not a priority — the game works fine if people
just come back daily without tracking their history.
